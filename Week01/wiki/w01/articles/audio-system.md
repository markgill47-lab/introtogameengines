# The Audio System

> **Covered:** Week 7 · **Organ of:** [[common-anatomy|the Common Anatomy]]

One-shots, loops, mixers, 3D positioning.

Four words, four ideas, and Week 7 unpacks each: a footstep is a one-shot; the tavern music is a loop; the mixer decides how loud each is relative to the others; and 3D positioning makes the blacksmith's hammer get louder as you walk toward it — the audio system reading positions out of the [[scene-system|scene tree]].

In the [[demo-game-w01|W01_Anatomy demo]], the audio station is exactly that last trick: a cube that hums, louder as the camera gets closer. One audio source, zero code.
