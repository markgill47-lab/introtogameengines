# The Animation System

> **Covered:** Week 8, with a callback in Week 10 you'll enjoy · **Organ of:** [[common-anatomy|the Common Anatomy]]

Data that changes properties over time, with state machines to decide which data plays.

Both halves of that sentence get their own week: the *data that changes properties* is Week 8, and the *state machines that decide* come back in Week 10 — the same one-state-at-a-time, clear-transitions machine that drives an enemy's brain (patrol → chase → attack) also decides whether your character is in the walk cycle or the jump.

Week 8 is also where you'll meet the [[asset-pipeline|asset pipeline]]'s rite of passage: the sideways, forty-times-too-large T-posing Mixamo character.
