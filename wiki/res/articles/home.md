# The Free Library

**There is no textbook for this course.** There are, instead, some excellent free ones, each assigned where it's strongest, plus a shelf of deeper material for when a topic hooks you. Everything here is legally free to read or watch: full texts posted by their authors, open licenses, or publicly posted talks.

Each weekly module ends with a "Going deeper" section pointing into this library; this wiki is the whole library in one place.

## How the shelves are organized

- **[[nystrom|The Core Stack]]**: the five texts assigned reading lives in. If the course had a required book, it would be [[nystrom|Game Programming Patterns]], and the B tier is standing on it.
- **[[red-blob|The Reference Shelf]]**: the places to go when a week's concept leaves you wanting the real math or a second explanation.
- **[[shaders|Deeper Water]]**: tutorial lane, final projects, and hooked students. Nothing here is assigned. All of it is where curiosity goes next.
- **[[reading-by-week|The Map]]**: which reading pairs with which week, in one table.

If you're wondering why there's no single comprehensive engine text on this list, that question has a real answer: [[wheres-the-textbook|Where's the Textbook?]]

## A closing note on the economics

Every item here is free because someone decided teaching the field mattered more than paywalling it. When one of these resources carries you somewhere, the professional courtesies are: cite them in your build notes, buy the paid copy when you can someday, and pay the habit forward.
