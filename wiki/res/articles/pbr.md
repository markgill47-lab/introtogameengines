# PBR Materials

What those Metallic and Smoothness sliders in your engine actually mean, derived.

- **[LearnOpenGL's PBR section](https://learnopengl.com/PBR/Theory)**: the standard free walkthrough of physically based rendering theory and implementation.
- **Google's [Filament PBR guide](https://google.github.io/filament/Filament.html)**: heavier but authoritative. The working notes of a production renderer, free.

## Where it pairs with the course

You'll set material properties from Week 2 onward without thinking about them, which is the [[w01:renderer|renderer]] doing its job. This shelf is for the student who wants to know what the sliders are *doing*: energy conservation, microfacets, why metals have no diffuse. Strong final-project-lane-4 territory.

*Start with [[shaders|Shaders]] first if you haven't; PBR theory lands better once you've written a fragment shader in anger.*
