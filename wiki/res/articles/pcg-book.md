# Procedural Content Generation in Games

> **Authors:** Shaker, Togelius, and Nelson · **Read it:** [pcgbook.com](https://www.pcgbook.com/) · **License:** author-version chapters posted free by explicit arrangement with the publisher

The academic standard on procgen, with all twelve author-version chapters posted free: constructive methods, fractals and noise, grammars, dungeons and levels, rules, evaluation. Academic but readable. Bonus: the authors' course slides are posted too.

## Where it pairs with the course

**Week 14's companion, and the tutorial lane's deep end.** Chapters 1 and 3 (constructive generation) and the dungeon chapter align directly with the demo game's mine.

**One caveat:** these are 2016 author versions. Solid for fundamentals, predates the neural-generation era, which this course doesn't teach anyway.
