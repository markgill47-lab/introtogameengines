# Artificial Intelligence and Games

> **Authors:** Yannakakis and Togelius · **Read it:** [gameaibook.org](https://gameaibook.org/) · **License:** complete Springer textbook with the full PDF posted free (an updated 2024 copy is posted too)

A complete Springer textbook, free. Covers behavior authoring (FSMs, behavior trees), pathfinding, and far beyond: player modeling, generation, machine learning. The rest goes far past this course, which is what shelves are for.

## Where it pairs with the course

**Week 15** uses its behavior-authoring sections for the decision and path layers, and it's the deep end for A-tier students. It's heavier and more academic than the course's register: assign sections, not chapters, and read it the same way.

*The FSMs it formalizes are the same machines Week 10 builds; you met the first one in [[w01:common-anatomy|Week 1's anatomy tour]] as a goblin's brain. For pathfinding illustrated interactively instead of derived, see [[red-blob|Red Blob Games]].*
