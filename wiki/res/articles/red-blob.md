# Red Blob Games

> **Author:** Amit Patel · **Read it:** [redblobgames.com](https://www.redblobgames.com/)

The best interactive explanations anywhere of A* and pathfinding, grids and hexes, and [making maps with noise](https://www.redblobgames.com/maps/terrain-from-noise/). Every page is an explorable: you drag things, the math responds, and the concept installs itself.

## Where it pairs with the course

When **Week 14** or **Week 15** makes you curious about the math, start here. The A* pages are Week 15's pathfinding section, illustrated; the noise pages are [[pcg-book|the PCG book's]] terrain chapters, made draggable.

*If you like learning this way, the course's own wiki widgets (the [[w02:local-vs-world|transform playground]], the [[w02:the-pivot|pivot demo]]) are cut from the same cloth, deliberately.*
