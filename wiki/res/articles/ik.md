# Inverse Kinematics and Procedural Animation

> **Author:** Alan Zucconi · **Read it:** [the IK series](https://www.alanzucconi.com/tag/inverse-kinematics/): [2D](https://www.alanzucconi.com/2018/05/02/ik-2d-1/), [3D](https://www.alanzucconi.com/2020/09/14/inverse-kinematics-in-3d/), and [procedural animation](https://www.alanzucconi.com/2017/04/17/procedural-animations/)

The free, readable path from Week 8's animation states to legs that place their own feet.

## Where it pairs with the course

**Week 8** teaches the [[w01:animation-system|animation system]]: keyframe data plus state machines deciding what plays. IK is the next floor up, where animation stops being playback and starts being computation. Spider legs that find footholds, hands that reach for the actual doorknob.

His whole site is a tutorial-lane menu by itself. Fair warning: students who start the procedural animation series have a way of turning it into their final project.
