# Sebastian Lague

> **Watch:** his YouTube series: procedural terrain, pathfinding, and a dozen "Coding Adventure" episodes

The best visual companions to **Weeks 14 and 15** in existence, free.

## Where it pairs with the course

Where [[pcg-book|the PCG book]] derives and [[red-blob|Red Blob]] lets you drag, Lague *builds*, on camera, with the missteps left in. Watching him grow a terrain generator or debug an A* implementation is the closest thing to pair-programming the A tier has.

A production note worth stealing: his videos are also a masterclass in narrating a technical build, which is a skill this course grades every single week. Watch one and notice how he explains decisions, not keystrokes. That's the bar your demo videos are reaching for.
