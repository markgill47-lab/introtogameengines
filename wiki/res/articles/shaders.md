# Shaders

Three free paths in, ordered by gentleness:

1. **[The Book of Shaders](https://thebookofshaders.com/)** (Vigo and Lowe): the gentle, gorgeous, in-browser introduction to fragment shaders. Read it with the editor open and your code running live.
2. **[Catlike Coding](https://catlikecoding.com/unity/tutorials/)**: the Unity-specific path. Rendering, shaders, compute, and procedural work, in famously careful prose.
3. **[LearnOpenGL's shader chapters](https://learnopengl.com/Getting-started/Shaders)**: the from-first-principles version.

## Where it pairs with the course

Nowhere, officially, and that's the point of [[home|Deeper Water]]: shaders are on the F24 tutorial list that feeds **final-project lane 4** (the in-depth technical tutorial), and they're the classic show-off layer on any final project. The [[w01:renderer|renderer]] you configure all semester is running these; this shelf is how you get underneath it.
