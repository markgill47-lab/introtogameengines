Openly licensed and conceptually excellent **even if you never touch Godot**: reading its explanations of scene trees, signals, and physics is a free second angle on half this course's [[w02:cross-engine-dictionary|dictionary tables]].

Everywhere there's a cross-engine table, this is the best "second dictionary." The scene tree pages restate [[w02:scene-graph|Week 2]]; the signals pages will make Week 11's Observer material feel like home turf; the physics pages are a preview of Week 5.
