# Game Feel, On Video

The two talks Week 7 is quietly built on, free on YouTube:

- ***Juice it or lose it*** (Jonasson and Purho)
- ***The Art of Screenshake*** (Nijman, Vlambeer)

Search those titles; watch both; they're twenty minutes each and they'll change how you see every game you play. Seriously.

## Where it pairs with the course

**Week 7** (cameras, juice, feedback) and **A5**. These are the canonical materials on game feel, and they're talks rather than texts because the subject is fundamentally about watching the same interaction get progressively juicier. No book does what the live demo in *Juice it or lose it* does in its first five minutes.

This is also the shelf's reminder that "[[wheres-the-textbook|no open text covers this]]" sometimes means the best material was never a text to begin with.
