# Gaffer On Games

> **Author:** Glenn Fiedler · **Read it:** [gafferongames.com](https://gafferongames.com/)

The classic articles on game timing and networking.

## Where it pairs with the course

**[Fix Your Timestep!](https://gafferongames.com/post/fix_your_timestep/)** is Week 3's two-clocks section, argued by the person who made the argument famous. Read the week's module first, then read him make the same case with the gloves off.

**The networked physics series** is where multiplayer curiosity should start. This course doesn't teach netcode, and that's on purpose: it's a course of its own. When the curiosity hits anyway (it will, usually around the Micro-Game), his curated [GameNetworkingResources](https://github.com/gafferongames/GameNetworkingResources) list is the rabbit hole, mapped.
