# The Physics Engine

> **Covered:** Week 5 · **Organ of:** [[common-anatomy|the Common Anatomy]]

A parallel simulation of forces, bodies, and collisions that the [[renderer|renderer]] takes pictures of.

> **Meanwhile, in 2002:** *If I wanted physics, I came up with the physics.*

That "parallel simulation" framing matters: the physics world and the rendered picture are separate things that the engine keeps in sync. Week 5 makes you feel the difference.

If you choose [[threejs|Three.js]], note that physics is one of the subsystems you assemble yourself, via a library like **rapier** or **cannon-es**. Not a Week 1 problem. It is Week 5's problem, and Future You should know it's coming.
