# Recording Your Assignment 2 Video

A shape that fits 2–5 minutes:

1. **The code (45 sec):** walk the movement expression. Say the units: speed per second, times seconds. Point at `normalized` and say why. *(Covers [[a2-spec|Requirement 1]].)*
2. **Continuous + discrete (45 sec):** walk and sprint (held), then the Space action (edge). Say which function backs each and why they're different. *(Requirement 2.)*
3. **The Proof (60–90 sec):** landmark run uncapped, then at 15 fps: same seconds, chunkier picture. Then [[broken-twin|the Broken Twin]] at both framerates, misbehaving on cue. Narrate the *why*, not just the what. *(Requirement 3.)*
4. **The Sun or equivalent (20 sec):** something moving on dt with no input, as evidence "per second" means per second everywhere. *(Requirement 4.)*
5. **Wrap (15 sec):** what fought you, what fixed it.

Then the build note: what AI produced, what you fixed, what you'd change. One paragraph. Done.

*Recording setup lives in [[w01:recording-setup|Week 1's wiki]] if you skipped it: OBS, display capture, audio input, 1080p.*
